#  Theme Sample v1  Mega-Wow-Trivia

## Palette
- Primary: Neon Purple #6B3FA0
- Secondary: Neon Blue #00BFFF
- Background: Deep Midnight #0A0A0F
- Text: White #FFFFFF
- Success: Green #4CAF50
- Error: Red #FF5252
- Warning: Amber #FFC107

## Typography
- Heading Font: Bold, futuristic enchanted style (placeholder: *System Bold*)
- Body Font: Clean, readable sans-serif (placeholder: *System Regular*)
- Mono Font: For code/rank IDs (placeholder: *Monospace*)

## Spacing
- xs: 4
- sm: 8
- md: 16
- lg: 24
- xl: 32

## Corners (Radius)
- Small: 8
- Medium: 16
- Large: 24

## Components (Design Rules)
- Screen Background: Always deep midnight (#0A0A0F).
- Buttons: Purple base  glowing blue hover/press. Rounded corners.
- Cards: Dark surfaces with subtle shadow, purple or blue highlights.
- Headings: Always glowing blue text with bold weight.
- Animations: Confetti bursts, glowing borders, maze shimmer effects.
